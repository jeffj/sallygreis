How to Prepare Your Propery

A Camera captures what is there, dust or baseboard dirt will usually not be seen but there are some areas that will stick out. Here are some tips to make the shoot a good one.

  • Remove knick·knacks: Get all knick·knack, stacks of paper, remote controls, extraneous furniture, family photos, pet stuff & toys out of sight.
  • Surfaces: The counter tops in the bathrooms and kitchen should be as clean and as empty as possible.
  • Bathroom: Hide all your personal bath and shower effects.
  • Bins and Cans: Hide the trash cans, power cords and cables as much as you can.
  • Lights: Replace any light bulbs that are out.
  • Upholstery: All bedding, comforters, pillows and bed skirts should be properly aligned.
  • Color: Bring some color; a flower arrangement on the dining room table or a large bathroom. Or a bowl of oranges, apples or lemons in the kitchen.
  • Outdoors: Have the lawn mowed, leaves raked, bushes trimmed.
  • Vehicles: Remove all vehicles from the driveway and front of the house.
